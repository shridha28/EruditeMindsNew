What do we do!!

We are a team of people who are passionate about four legged creatures on this earth. We strive for their rescue to make earth a better place to live in. Saving one animal will not change the world, but surely for that one animal, the world will change forever!!

Fall in love

Whoever declared that love at first sight doesn’t exist has never witnessed the purity of an animal or looked deep into a four legged’s eyes. A human to an animal relationship is inexplicable, it cannot be described but only felt. We are to help you develop this wonderful relationship, and help you realise the importance of animals on this earth.

Adopt an animal today

We feel that until one has loved an animal,a part of one's soul remains unawakened.Rescue animals aren't broken, they've simply experienced more life than other animals. If they were human, we would call them wise. They would be the ones with tales to tell and stories to write, the ones dealt a bad hand who responded with courage. Don't pity them. Do something. Help to Donate. Volunteer. Foster. Adopt. And be proud to have their greatness by your side.

We chirp too

We are not limited to only four legged,but we are also a team in love with feathered creatures.Birds are an important part of our ecosystem.It is indeed a divine feeling to see the sun arising, darkness evaporating and the singing birds coming from nowhere. It is,we as humans,our utmost responsibility to protect and preserve these wonderful feathered creatures.

Pledge to protect our Wildlife

No matter how few possessions you own or how little money you have, loving wildlife and nature will make you rich beyond measure.Never apologize for being over sensitive and emotional when defending the welfare of wildlife. Let this be a sign that you have a big heart and aren't afraid to show your true feelings. These emotions give you the strength to fight for what is right and to be the voice of those who cannot be heard.
